Our Team

is a bunch of goobers from the Midwest, USA

We're friends. We're family. We make art, music, books, and games (both tabletop and virtual) with fun at the heart of everything we do.

  • Wren: code, digital stuff, database enthusiast
  • Cal: game design, layouts, talkative rubber duck
  • Robyn: graphic design, card art, illustrations, bird handler
  • Brian: writer, story teller, plot twist generator
  • Aaron: game design, programming, music, GM for tiny animals
  • Madeline: author, reader, editor, book...er, gluten hater
